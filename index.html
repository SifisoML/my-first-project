About Me

I work at the intersection of real estate transactions, credit risk, and investment analysis, with exposure to how deals are sourced, evaluated, and structured before capital is deployed.


Currently, I work in real estate acquisitions with Pebble Ridge Capital Group, a U.S. private equity firm focused on self-storage and industrial assets. My role sits at the front end of the acquisition process, where I screen investment opportunities, gather and analyze deal documentation, and prepare assets for underwriting and investment committee review.


Prior to this, I worked as a Business Development Specialist at Stratafin, originating and structuring property-backed funding transactions for sectional title schemes and homeowners' associations. The role involved assessing financial statements, levy rolls, arrears profiles, and governance structures before applications were escalated to credit committee.


This experience provided practical insight into how lenders evaluate risk, security, and cash-flow durability, and how transaction structure ultimately determines whether a deal is bankable.


Earlier in my career at IGrow Wealth Investments, I was involved in property investment consulting and development support, which provided exposure to portfolio structuring, investor advisory, and the operational side of residential property investment.


Across these roles, I've developed a strong interest in deal-driven finance, particularly real estate acquisitions and investment analysis, where sourcing, underwriting, and capital allocation intersect.


Alongside my professional work, I am completing a BCompt in Accounting Science (Management Accounting) to deepen my technical foundation as I continue building toward a long-term career in investment and asset management.
